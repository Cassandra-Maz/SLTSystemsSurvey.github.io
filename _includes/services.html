You're a good fit to take the survey if you...

are d/Deaf

People with any level of hearing loss may participate.

Have d/Deaf family members

This includes CODAs [Child of d/Deaf adult(s)], grandparents, grandchildren, siblings, cousins, etc...

Sign Regularly

This includes interpreters and learners. This study focuses primarily on ASL.

are 18+

Due to IRB permissions, only people 18 years or older can participate.
